## Design Style Options

I can create completely different design approaches. Here are some modern, unique styles:

### 1. **Glassmorphism / Neumorphism Style**
- **Characteristics:**
  - Frosted glass effects with backdrop blur
  - Soft shadows and depth
  - Subtle borders with transparency
  - Card-based layouts with glass panels
  - Modern iOS/macOS Big Sur aesthetic
  
- **Color Scheme:** Soft pastels, white/light backgrounds with dark mode option
- **Best For:** Modern, premium feel, clean and minimal

### 2. **Brutalist / Bold Design**
- **Characteristics:**
  - Bold typography, strong contrasts
  - Geometric shapes, sharp edges
  - High contrast colors
  - Asymmetric layouts
  - Bold borders, no rounded corners (or very minimal)
  
- **Color Scheme:** High contrast (black/white/one accent color)
- **Best For:** Edgy, modern, stands out, unique identity

### 3. **Minimalist / Clean Design**
- **Characteristics:**
  - Lots of white space
  - Simple typography hierarchy
  - Subtle shadows, minimal borders
  - Focus on content
  - Clean lines, organized sections
  
- **Color Scheme:** Neutral grays, one accent color
- **Best For:** Professional, easy to read, timeless

### 4. **Card-Based / Pinterest-Style**
- **Characteristics:**
  - Grid layouts with cards
  - Hover effects and shadows
  - Image-focused
  - Masonry layouts possible
  - Clean card designs
  
- **Color Scheme:** White cards on colored/gradient backgrounds
- **Best For:** Visual content, discovery, browsing

### 5. **Gradient / Modern Colorful**
- **Characteristics:**
  - Vibrant gradients
  - Colorful backgrounds
  - Modern animations
  - Bold color choices
  - Playful but professional
  
- **Color Scheme:** Multiple vibrant colors, gradients
- **Best For:** Energetic, modern, engaging

### 6. **Dark Mode Premium / Luxury**
- **Characteristics:**
  - Deep dark backgrounds
  - Gold/teal/cyan accents
  - Premium feel
  - Elegant typography
  - Sophisticated spacing
  
- **Color Scheme:** Deep blacks, rich accent colors
- **Best For:** Premium feel, modern, sophisticated

### 7. **Material Design 3 / Google Style**
- **Characteristics:**
  - Material Design principles
  - Floating action buttons
  - Elevation and shadows
  - Ripple effects
  - Organized sections with clear hierarchy
  
- **Color Scheme:** Material color palette
- **Best For:** Familiar, organized, Google ecosystem feel

### 8. **Split Screen / Asymmetric Layout**
- **Characteristics:**
  - Split screen layouts
  - Asymmetric designs
  - Large hero sections
  - Sidebar navigation
  - Unique layouts per page
  
- **Color Scheme:** Contrasting sections
- **Best For:** Unique, modern, stands out
